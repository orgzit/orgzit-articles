For any business, organization, or team, insightful reports and dashboards are extremely important. There are metrics that every team wants to track on a regular basis. For helping out organizations gather valuable insights from their data and help them track their system, make decisions, and constantly be informed, Orgzit provides a feature to create and maintain reports.

Using Orgzit, you can [create](/articles/how-to-generate-reports) different types of reports. You can visualize your data in the form of Bar Graphs, Line Charts, Pie Charts, Tabular Reports, and Funnels. You can use these for different use cases and also look at these reports and insights regularly in a [dashboard](/articles/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets). Once your report is created, you can edit it, change its display type, and download it whenever required.

![](/images/explore-reports-in-orgzit/img-1.gif)

You can view the reports within a table and can also create widgets to look at the reports and insights on the dashboard. You can create [multiple dashboards](/articles/how-to-use-multiple-dashboards) for different types of reports. For eg. you can have a sales dashboard for the reports that depict your sales funnel and a marketing dashboard, that shows widgets with insights from different marketing campaigns of your organization. You can also [change the layout](/articles/how-to-change-the-layout-of-the-dashboard-on-orgzit) of your dashboard whenever you want.
