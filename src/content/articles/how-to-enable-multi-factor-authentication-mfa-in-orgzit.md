Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is a robust security feature offered by Orgzit that adds an extra layer of protection to your account. By enabling MFA in Orgzit, you significantly enhance the security measures in place, ensuring the integrity of your data and safeguarding against unauthorized access.

One of the key benefits of MFA is its ability to prevent account takeovers. Even if your password is compromised or stolen, the additional layer of authentication required through MFA acts as a strong deterrent. Attackers would need your password and the second-factor authentication, such as the OTP generated by your authenticator app, to access your Orgzit account. This effectively mitigates the risk of unauthorized individuals infiltrating your account and ensures that only authorized users can access your sensitive data and resources.

Follow the steps outlined in this guide to easily enable MFA using authenticator apps like Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator. Enjoy enhanced account security and have peace of mind knowing that your data in Orgzit is well-protected.

### Prerequisites before you begin the setup:

Before you begin, install an authenticator app on a smartphone or tablet (e.g., Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator).

Note: Keep your authenticator app and device secure. If you lose your device or uninstall the app, please contact the Orgzit Support team to temporarily disable MFA until you regain access to your account.

### Step 1: Access My Profile

1.  Log in to your Orgzit account using your credentials.
    
2.  Go to the "My Profile" section.
    

![](/images/how-to-enable-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-in-orgzit/img-1.png)

### Step 2: Enable MFA

1.  Within the "My Profile" section, locate the "MFA Authentication" section.
    
2.  Click on the "Generate OTP" button.
    

![](/images/how-to-enable-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-in-orgzit/img-2.png)

### Step 3: Set Up Authenticator App

1.  A pop-up window will appear with a QR code and instructions.
    
2.  On your smartphone or tablet, open the authenticator app.
    
3.  Use the app's QR code scanner to scan the QR code displayed in Orgzit.
    

![](/images/how-to-enable-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-in-orgzit/img-3.png)

### Step 4: Obtain and Enter OTP

1.  Once the QR code is scanned, the authenticator app will generate a One-Time Password (OTP) for Orgzit.
    
2.  Enter the OTP into the designated field in the Orgzit web app.
    
3.  Click on the "Submit" button to complete the MFA setup.
    

![](/images/how-to-enable-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-in-orgzit/img-4.png)

Congratulations! You have successfully enabled Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) in Orgzit. Moving forward, when you log in to your Orgzit account, you will need to provide the OTP generated by your authenticator app in addition to your username and password.

### Step 5: Login Process with MFA

1.  Visit the Orgzit login page.
    
2.  Enter your Email and password as usual.
    
3.  Open your authenticator app and locate the OTP generated for Orgzit.
    
4.  Enter the OTP in the MFA Authentication Page.
    
5.  Click on “Verify”, once the OTP is verified, you will gain access to your Orgzit account.
    

![](/images/how-to-enable-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-in-orgzit/img-5.gif)
