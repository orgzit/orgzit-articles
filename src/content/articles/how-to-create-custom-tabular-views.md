Orgzit provides different types of [views](/articles/explore-views-in-orgzit) to present the user's data. Users can also create **custom tabular views** in their Orgzit workspace. They can customize it with the data fields they want in a view by using the [show/hide fields](/articles/how-to-show-hide-fields) feature.

To create **Custom Tabular Views**, the following steps have to be performed:

*   Click on the **Show/Hide Fields** button available in your Table and select the fields/columns you want to be **shown** in your custom view.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-custom-tabular-views/img-1.png)

*   Once you select the fields that you want to see, you can **save** it as a new view for future use by clicking on the Save View button.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-custom-tabular-views/img-2.jpeg)

*   You can **name** your custom view and save it.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-custom-tabular-views/img-3.png)

*   You can see your newly made custom view in the list of views. You can also **edit** or **delete** a custom view if required.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-custom-tabular-views/img-4.jpeg)

*   You can also choose the **default view** for every table. To do so, move to the **table settings**, and in the **[table details](/articles/how-to-set-up-the-table-details) section** choose the **default view** for your table.  
      
    The default view is the view that you'll see every time you open this table.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-custom-tabular-views/img-5.jpeg)
