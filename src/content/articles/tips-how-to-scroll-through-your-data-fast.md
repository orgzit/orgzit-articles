Scrolling using the scroll bar can be sometimes be frustrating especially when it comes to move from left to right and vice- versa.  
   
With this simple browser shortcut, you can easily scroll horizontally through your data on Orgzit - especially the tabular. 

Follow the below given steps to scroll with ease:

1.  Got to a Workspace 
    
2.  Go to a Table
    
3.  Click on 'shift' on your keyboard and scroll with the centre button of your computer mouse to go from left to right and vice- versa.
