After you have signed up and completed the one time onboarding process, you can log in to your account by following this simple process:

1.  While signing up, we request you to provide an email ID, where the welcome emails from our team are sent. The username (part of the email id without the domain name) of your email id is used to generate your Orgzit account URL.        For eg: You registered with the email ID [orgzit3@gmail.com.](mailto:orgzit3@gmail.com) You can access your Orgzit account via the URL: `orgzit3.orgzit.com`  
    [You can use the same email ID with multiple accounts.](/articles/can-i-sign-in-up-multiple-accounts-using-the-same-email-id)  
    ​
    
2.  When redirected, you will be prompted to enter your login information which consists of the email ID you used to register and the password you set via the welcome mail sent by our team.  
    ​
    
3.  Click on Log In and you will be redirected to your personal dashboard.
