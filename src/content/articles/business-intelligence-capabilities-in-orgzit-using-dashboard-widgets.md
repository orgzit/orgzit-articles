Dashboard Widgets enable users to quickly look at important [reports](/articles/how-to-generate-reports) and insights. They allow you to access reports from the different tables of a workspace on a dashboard. Using widgets, you can view the progress of work in the different apps of your project at a glance in one place.  
  
​

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-1.png)

  
You can also create and [manage multiple dashboards](/articles/how-to-use-multiple-dashboards) within your Orgzit account ( on the Manage Account page ) and can have different widgets on different dashboards. For instance, you can have a separate _Sales Dashboard_ with widgets of your Sales related reports and a separate _Marketing Dashboard_ to track your marketing metrics.  
  
_Note: Dashboard Widgets can only be created for existing [reports](/articles/how-to-generate-reports)._

# **Adding a New Widget**

*   Users can create new widgets on their dashboard by clicking on the "**\+ New Widget**" button on the dashboard. Remember, these widgets are only created for that specific user.
    

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-2.jpeg)

*   Alternatively, the [account admin can create new widgets from the **manage account** page](/articles/how-to-manage-account-widgets-for-all-users). The Admin can control the [roles](/articles/how-to-manage-different-roles-in-a-workspace) that can access the added widgets. These widgets can be added to different dashboards and be accessed by the users specified.  
      
    For this, click on your profile picture at the top right corner of your workspace screen and click on **Manage Account**.  
    ​
    

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-3.jpeg)

  
Move to the **Account Widgets** section and click on + **add widget**.  
​

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-4.png)

To add a new widget by any one of the above-mentioned methods, the following steps have to be performed:

1.  Click on the **Add Widget** button.
    
2.  Select the **type of report.**  
    Choose the report type as _single-valued_ if you want to look at a specific number. For example, the number of follow-ups scheduled today.  
    Select the type as a _multiple value chart_ if you want to look at the visualization of your report in the form of a graph.
    
3.  Select the **workspace** and the **report** for which you want to create a widget.
    
4.  Enter the **name** of the widget that you want to create.
    
5.  Select the **roles** to apply the widget to (in case of Admin created widgets from the Account Manage page).
    
6.  Select the **type of icon** that you feel is best suitable for your widget.
    
7.  Choose a **color** for your icon.
    
8.  Select the **dashboard** on which you want your widget to appear. You can manage multiple dashboards in Orgzit.
    
9.  Click on **create**. A new widget is now created on the dashboard that you chose.
    

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-5.png)

The Admin can publish, unpublish, edit, or delete a widget whenever required, from the Manage Account page.

## A list of options is available on all dashboard widgets for performing operations on the reports. Here is the list of report buttons and their uses:

![](/images/business-intelligence-capabilities-in-orgzit-using-dashboard-widgets/img-6.png)

**Change Filter:** You can use this button to apply [filters](/articles/how-to-filter-data-in-orgzit) to your report. You can quickly apply conditions, filter your data, and look at the report built on your filtered data.

**Apply Saved Filter**: This shows you a drop-down of all the [saved filters](/articles/how-do-saved-filters-work) that you can apply to your report.

**Show Total**: This shows the sum of the different report values.

**Change Display Type:** This button can help you if you want to change the display type of your report from one to another.

**Resize**: Using this button, you can increase or decrease the size of your dashboard widget.

**Download**: This button gives you the option to download, save, annotate, and print the report in the required format.

**Refresh**: Clicking on this button will refresh your report.

**Remove**: Using the remove button, you can remove a widget from your dashboard.

# **Change your Dashboard Layout**

The **lock/unlock** toggle button for dashboards is used to change the dashboard layout.  
  
Once you unlock it, you can drag and drop your dashboard widgets to change their position on the dashboard. Once you place the widgets as per your need, you can freeze the placement by clicking on the toggle button to lock it.
