At Orgzit, maintaining data integrity is our priority.  Orgzit uses 256-BIT SSL encryption to ensure all information between customer's computers and our servers are secure and encrypted. Our servers are physically located with Amazon Web Services, who provide best-of-breed physical security. We keep all server security patches up to date.
