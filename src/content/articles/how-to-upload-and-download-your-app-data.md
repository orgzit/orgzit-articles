You can easily upload or download CSV files in Orgzit. Here’s how:

**Uploading CSV files**

Go to your Table where you want to upload data CSV. Click on the **gear** icon and then on **Upload CSV**. Choose a file and hit **Submit**.

**Note:** You will only be able to upload a CSV if the app and the CSV contain the same fields and data types.

This video can be your guide to uploading a CSV.

**Downloading CSV files**

Go to the app page of the app that you wish to download in the form of a CSV. Click on the **gear** icon and then on **Download CSV**.

Here's a video guide to help you download your data from Orgzit.

**Note:**  
This feature will download all fields, irrespective of whether they have been hidden.  
If filters have been applied to your app data, then this feature will download all fields for the filtered data.
