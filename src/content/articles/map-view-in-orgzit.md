Map view is another amazing feature provided by Orgzit. This helps you locate and have an overall view of all the resources you have deployed or your assets present in the field in one go. 

To utilize the map view, you can follow these steps:

1.  Add a field to an app with its data type as "location"
    
2.  New field will be displayed in the app. Double click on any record's location field and enter the desired location. 
    
3.  Repeat step 2 for adding map locations to other records
    
4.  Click on the "Views" button on the top left corner of the app. A new view will be listed in the views menu by your field's name.
    
5.  Click on the view and your map will be loaded.
    
6.  All the records against which locations have been added will be listed here. You can directly click on "view record" by hovering your mouse over the location point.  
      
    **_NOTE: This is static Map data, and not real time Map data._**
