# Understanding Orgzit's project types

There are two different kinds of workspaces that you can create in Orgzit. Workspace types vary on the basis of:

1.  The objective of the Workspace
    
2.  Who needs access to the Workspace
    

Let's take a closer look at the twp types of Workspaces:

# Public Workspaces 

"Team projects" are projects that you create for a specific team. You may, for example, create a Compensation or Recruitment project for your Human Resource team. Once you create a team project, every member of your team will be able to view and join it.

# My Workspaces

When you create a new Workspace but do not define a team for it, you create what is known as a "My Workspace". "My Workspace" can be created by users to track personal projects and tasks, which they do not wish to share with other Orgzit members.
