Orgzit gives you the power and flexibility to organize and manage data in the way you want!

To make the process more convenient, Orgzit enables you to work with data from your existing CSV files. It does so by uploading the file directly into your workspace in a new table.

To create a new table by uploading a CSV, the following three steps have to be performed:

1.  Click on the **"+"** after the last tab for the workspace tables to add a new table.
    

![](/images/how-to-create-a-new-table-using-csv-upload/img-1.png)

2\. Choose the **Upload a CSV** option.

![](/images/how-to-create-a-new-table-using-csv-upload/img-2.png)

3\. Choose the CSV file that you want to upload and click on the “Submit” button.

![](/images/how-to-create-a-new-table-using-csv-upload/img-3.png)

4\. Verify the datatypes of all the fields and make changes, if required. Once verified, click on the “Finish” button.

![](/images/how-to-create-a-new-table-using-csv-upload/img-4.png)

A new table with the name of your CSV is now added to your respective workspace. By using this feature, you can quickly transfer data from your CSV to your workspace at Orgzit and get started!
