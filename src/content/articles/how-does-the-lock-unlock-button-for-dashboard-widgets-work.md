The lock/unlock toggle button for dashboards is used to change the dashboard layout. Once you unlock it, you can drag and drop your [dashboard widgets](/articles/how-to-add-a-new-widget) to change their position on the dashboard. Once you place the widgets as per your need, you can freeze the placement by clicking on the toggle button to lock it.

![](/images/how-does-the-lock-unlock-button-for-dashboard-widgets-work/img-1.gif)

**Dashboard widgets** help you view your [reports](/articles/explore-reports-in-orgzit) from the different parts of your business at a glance so that you can quickly get a snapshot of your business data. You can also use the different [report buttons](/articles/what-are-the-different-report-buttons-used-for) on the widgets to perform operations like, changing the display type of report, filtering report data, etc.  
​

A great advantage of the **Orgzit dashboard** is that it is highly customizable, which means you can add different types of widgets, pin your reports, tasks, etc and arrange them in a layout that works best for you.  
  
You can view the [list of account widgets](/articles/how-to-manage-account-widgets-for-all-users) in the Account Manage page. You can publish, unpublish, edit or delete your widgets from there.
