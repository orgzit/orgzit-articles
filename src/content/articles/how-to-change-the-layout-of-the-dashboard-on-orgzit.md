Orgzit dashboards help you view your reports from the different parts of your business at a glance so that you can quickly get a snapshot of your business data. 

A great advantage about the Orgzit dashboard is that it is highly customizable, which means you can add different types of widgets, pin your reports, tasks etc and arrange them in a layout that works best for you. 

Follow these steps to change the layout of your Orgzit dashboard:

1.  Unlock your dashboard
    
2.  Drag and place your data as per your requirement
    
3.  Lock your dashboard so that your changes are saved
