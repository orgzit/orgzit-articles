In the full-page detailed record view, you can view all data to do with a particular record on one screen.  
  
​

![](/images/how-to-expand-a-record-to-a-full-page-detail-view/img-1.png)

  
The full-page detailed record view has three main parts:

1.  **Record details:** All data under the record is visible.
    
2.  **[Collaboration features](/articles/collaboration-features-in-orgzit):** Comments, reminders, files, interactions, and history can be added and viewed.
    
3.  **Related Tables:** Related Tables, if any, are displayed at the bottom of the page.  
    ​
    

**To access the full-page detailed record view, click on the View Record split icon at the left end of the record.**  
  
​
