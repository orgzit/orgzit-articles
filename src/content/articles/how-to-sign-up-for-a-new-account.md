At Orgzit, we have made our on-boarding process as user friendly as possible. Signing up with Orgzit allows you to use our product for a period of two weeks, during which you will have access to all the features of out product. Most importantly, this process doesn't require any credit card information or IT setup.  
  
Steps to a smooth sign up process are:  
​

1.  To sign up for a new account you need to visit our website [www.orgzit.com](https://orgzit.com) .
    
2.  On opening the website, scroll down and you will see a field where you can share your email ID and click on the button ("Start your free trial") next to it.
    
3.  After signing up, you will be redirected to a page where you can select a pre-defined solution designed by our team, as per your business needs.
    
4.  You can now also invite your Team Members on to Orgzit (free of any restrictions for the 14 day trial)
    
5.  You will also receive a couple of welcome emails from us. One of them will be about the information on your new account.
