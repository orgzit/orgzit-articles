The Orgzit dashboard helps you manage and view your data at a glance easily. It is highly customizable and you can also use multiple dashboards while on Orgzit.

Once you have access to multiple dashboards on Orgzit, here is how you can use them:

1.   Click on the dashboard dropdown
    
2.  Select the dashboard you want to view.
