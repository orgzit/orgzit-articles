Orgzit offers a 2 week trial period for all the users to get acquainted with the platform. Post the trial period, you will have the option of upgrading your package from trial mode to a paid version.  
  
Steps to do so are as follows:

1.  Login to your Orgzit account
    
2.  You will see a  button on the top right corner of the dashboard. The button will be marked as an "upward arrow" with the message, **Upgrade Plan**.  
      
    ​
    
    ![](/images/how-to-upgrade-the-pricing-plan-after-the-trial-period-or-from-super-teams-to-premium/img-1.png)
    
3.  Clicking on it will present a dialog box that will have the options of available upgrades. 
    
4.  If you are using a trial, you will have the option of upgrading to a **Starter** plan or a **Super Teams** plan.  
      
    Pricing details are given according to the type of billing cycle you opt for, i.e. billed monthly or annually.  
    ​
    
    ![](/images/how-to-upgrade-the-pricing-plan-after-the-trial-period-or-from-super-teams-to-premium/img-2.png)
    
5.  Similarly, if you are an existing paid user of the **Super Teams** plan, you will have the option of upgrading to a **Premium** plan.
    
6.  To upgrade, you will be requested to share your phone number and the number of users you plan to have on our platform.
    
7.  Once you have entered the requisite details, you can click on **Send Request** and our team will get in touch with you.
    

You can find the [latest pricing details on our website's pricing page here](https://orgzit.com/pricing/).

To know more details or to discuss the upgrade, you can connect with our Sales Team at [sales@orgzit.com](mailto:sales@orgzit.com)
