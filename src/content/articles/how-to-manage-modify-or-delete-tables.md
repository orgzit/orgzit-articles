Tables in a Workspace can be modified from the Manage Table page, accessed by clicking the **gear** icon on the Table page. On the Manage app page, you can alter app name, general app attributes, fields and also manage reports and workflows.

You can **delete** a Table by clicking on the red **Delete Table** button on the Manage Table page.
