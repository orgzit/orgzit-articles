Orgzit provides many features that help increase the overall productivity and efficiency of your system and processes. One such feature is the **bulk edit** option for your records in Orgzit.

Many times, we want to edit the value of a field(s) for all records. **For instance,** you have a Table with the _Monthly Payroll_ records for each of your Employees. Once their salary has been _processed,_ you want to change the _Payroll Status_ of all employees, from _Processing_ to _Processed_. Now, changing the value of each employee's _Payroll Status_ manually would require a lot of effort and time. But with the help of the **Bulk Edit** feature, you can change in one go!

**To bulk edit records in Orgzit, the following steps need to be performed:**

![](/images/how-to-bulk-edit-records/img-1.jpeg)

1.  Click on the **Bulk Edit** button at the top-left corner of your table.
    
2.  In the blank fields created, specify the **value** you want to be assigned to that field **in all rows**.
    
3.  Once specified for as many fields as you want to edit, click on the **green-colored submit icon** to submit the changes made.  
      
    {In the image below, the _discount type_ for all items is being updated in bulk.}
    

![](/images/how-to-bulk-edit-records/img-2.jpeg)

By using the above-mentioned steps, you can bulk edit records in Orgzit as per your requirement!
