In the Invoicing and Order Management Workspace, all the information relevant to managing your orders and serving your customers are systematically connected to ensure that your order management workflows are smooth and seamless. 

Following is a quick how-to guide to help you understand how to track payments from your customers as part of the Invoice and Order Management solution.  
Switch tables by clicking on the “Payments received” table.

*   Close to the top right corner of your screen, you will see a button called "_+Payment received_". 
    

![](/images/how-to-add-a-payment-record/img-2.png)

*   Clicking on this button will open a popup with a form for adding a payment.
    
*   On the “Create Payment” form, you need to fill the data in the respective fields. Some of these fields can be filled from the drop-down menu available against the field. For eg: The field called "Sales order" will have the list of all the existing sales order. Similarly, the field “mode of payment”
    
*   Fields with a red star next to them are required fields. Be sure to fill them or else you will get an error when you create the payment record.
    
*   Click on the "_Create_" button to create a payment check.
